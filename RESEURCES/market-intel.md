# Market Intelligence Page

## Meta
**Title** Market Intelligence — Volatility Heatmaps Session Activity News Impact

---

## Features
**Volatility Heatmaps** visualize intraday volatility by symbol and session  
**Session Activity** liquidity and volume snapshots by session  
**News Impact Tracker** historical performance around scheduled news events  
**Correlation Tracking** symbol correlation matrix and regime changes

---

## Trader Use Cases
- Identify sessions where your strategy historically performs best  
- Avoid pairs with high negative correlation to your core positions during risk events
