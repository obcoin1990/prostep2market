# Brand Assets

## Logo
**Primary Mark** full logo with left graphic and stacked text 'PROSTEP' / '2MARKET'  
**Lockups** horizontal lockup (graphic left, text right); stacked lockup (graphic above text)  
**Clearspace** maintain at least 1x the height of the '2' in 2MARKET around the mark  
**Minimum Size** 48px wide for digital; 12mm for print

## File formats to keep
- SVG (primary for web and print)  
- PNG 2x and 3x for raster needs  
- PDF for print-ready assets

## Variants
- **Full color** red + black + green on white background  
- **Reversed** white text and green graphic on black background  
- **Monochrome** single-color black or white for constrained use
