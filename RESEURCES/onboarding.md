# Onboarding Flow

## Meta
**Title** Onboarding Flow — Trader DNA MT5 Setup Experience Questionnaire

---

## Step 1 Create Account
**Copy** Quick sign up with email or social login. Verify email to unlock onboarding.

---

## Step 2 Trader DNA Assessment
**Purpose** Build a personalized profile to tailor coaching and analytics.  
**Structure**
- **Section 1** Risk Personality questions  
- **Section 2** Emotional Stability scenarios  
- **Section 3** Decision Making Style situational choices  
- **Section 4** Trading Behavior habits and history  
- **Section 5** Learning Style preferences  
**Outcome** Trader profile summary and recommended learning path

---

## Step 3 Experience Questionnaire
**Questions**
- Years trading; preferred instruments; average trade duration; typical RR; daily trading hours  
**Outcome** Baseline metrics for benchmarking

---

## Step 4 Risk Profile Setup
**Inputs**
- Account size; max risk per trade; max daily risk; max drawdown tolerance  
**Outputs**
- Default risk guard thresholds and alert settings

---

## Step 5 MT5 Connection Setup
**Options**
- **MT5 Read Only** step by step connection guide with screenshots  
- **Manual Upload** CSV template and example  
**Security Note** Only read only connection is required; no trading permissions requested

---

## Step 6 Initial Dashboard Tour
**Highlights**
- Edge Score; Emotional Risk Meter; Weekly Performance; AI Alerts  
**CTA** Start Journal
