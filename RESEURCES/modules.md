## Module Overview
**Headline** Six Core Systems That Form the Operating System for Retail Trader Growth  
**Copy** Each module is designed to target a specific dimension of trader development from psychology to execution quality.

---

## Module 1 Trader DNA System
**Description** A comprehensive psychological and behavioral assessment completed at onboarding to personalize education, AI coaching, and risk monitoring.  
**Assessment Categories**
- **Risk Personality** aggression level; risk appetite; loss tolerance; recovery behavior  
- **Emotional Stability** impulsiveness; patience; frustration response; revenge tendencies  
- **Decision Making Style** analytical trader; emotional trader; reactive trader; structured trader  
- **Trading Behavior** overtrading probability; FOMO tendencies; discipline level; consistency habits  
- **Learning Style** visual learner; structured learner; practical learner  
**Outputs**
- **Trader Profile** personality type such as The Sniper, The Analyst, The Warrior  
- **Personalized Recommendations** learning path, warnings, dashboard layout

---

## Module 2 AI Trade Intelligence Engine
**Description** Automated analysis of trade history and journal entries to measure trade quality, risk, and emotional impact.  
**Inputs** MT5 read only connection; manual uploads; CSV imports; journal entries  
**Analyses**
- **Trade Quality** entry quality; exit quality; RR efficiency; timing analysis  
- **Emotional Analysis** revenge trading; impulsive trading; overconfidence; fear based exits  
- **Risk Analysis** lot size inconsistency; drawdown behavior; margin pressure; overexposure  
- **Performance Analysis** best sessions; worst market conditions; pair performance  
**Example Feedback**
- **Insight** Your win rate decreases after consecutive losses.  
- **Action** Pause trading after two consecutive losses and review journal.

---

## Module 3 Risk Guardian System
**Description** Continuous behavioral monitoring that issues non intrusive warnings when risk or emotional patterns escalate.  
**Capabilities**
- **Behavioral Alerts** revenge trading alert; fatigue alert; risk escalation alert; emotional instability alert  
- **Non Intrusive** never executes trades or gives signals; only provides coaching and warnings  
**Use Cases**
- **Auto Pause Suggestion** recommend break after extended sessions  
- **Exposure Warning** notify when current exposure exceeds normal profile

---

## Module 4 Trade Journal System
**Description** Professional journaling with AI review to identify recurring mistakes and emotional triggers.  
**Features**
- **Trade Upload** symbol; entry; exit; SL; TP; lot size; session; result  
- **Screenshot Attachments** visual evidence for review  
- **Emotional Tracking** confidence; stress; emotional state  
- **AI Reflection** repeated mistakes; discipline scoring; improvement suggestions

---

## Module 5 Edge Score System
**Description** Gamified performance ranking that quantifies trader skill across multiple dimensions.  
**Score Components**
- **Discipline Score** rule adherence and journaling consistency  
- **Risk Score** position sizing and drawdown control  
- **Emotional Stability Score** measured emotional responses and recovery  
- **Consistency Score** streaks and variance in returns  
- **Strategy Adherence Score** following pre defined rules  
**Ranks**
- Beginner; Developing Trader; Consistent Trader; Advanced Trader; Elite Trader

---

## Module 6 Education and Strategy Lab
**Description** Structured learning combined with a sandbox to test strategies and behavioral rules.  
**Education**
- **Courses** psychology; risk management; market structure; institutional concepts  
- **Certifications** completion badges and certificates  
**Strategy Lab**
- **Simulations** session based trading; RR variations; news avoidance strategies  
- **Metrics** simulated performance; drawdown; consistency; behavioral impact
