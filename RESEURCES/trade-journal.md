# Trade Journal Page

## Meta
**Title** Trade Journal — Upload Review Emotional Tracking AI Reflection

---

## Journal Features
**Trade Upload** CSV import; manual entry; MT5 sync  
**Screenshot Upload** attach chart images and trade context  
**Emotional Notes** log confidence; stress; triggers; pre trade plan adherence  
**AI Review** automated identification of repeated mistakes and emotional triggers  
**Journal Timeline** chronological view with filters by symbol; session; emotion

---

## AI Journal Outputs
**Recurring Mistakes** list with frequency and examples  
**Behavioral Triggers** events that precede poor decisions  
**Discipline Score** daily and weekly scoring with trendline  
**Action Plan** prioritized steps to fix recurring issues

---

## Best Practices
**Guidance**
- Log trades within 24 hours  
- Attach at least one screenshot for losing trades  
- Rate pre trade plan adherence on every trade
