# Strategy Lab Page

## Meta
**Title** Strategy Lab — Simulate Optimize Compare Behavioral Impact

---

## Lab Features
**Custom Strategy Builder** define entry rules; exit rules; RR; position sizing rules  
**Session Testing** run strategies across session windows and market regimes  
**RR Optimization** test multiple RR combinations and measure consistency  
**Behavioral Comparison** simulate same strategy with different behavioral rules such as stop trading after X losses  
**Performance Metrics** simulated PnL; max drawdown; consistency score; behavioral risk

---

## Use Cases
- Test news avoidance strategies and measure drawdown reduction  
- Compare fixed lot sizing versus Kelly based sizing under your risk profile  
- Simulate the effect of forced breaks after losing streaks
