**Title** AI Analysis — Trade Quality Emotional Risk Pattern Detection

## Features
**Trade Breakdown** entry and exit quality scoring; RR efficiency  
**Behavior Analysis** revenge trading detection; impulsive trade flags  
**Risk Analysis** exposure heatmap; lot size variance; drawdown triggers  
**Pattern Detection** recurring setups that correlate with losses or wins  
**Exportable Reports** PDF summary for coaching sessions

## Example Insights
**Insight** Your exit quality drops during high volatility news events.  
**Recommendation** Avoid trading 15 minutes before and after major news for this symbol.
