## Frequently Asked Questions
**What does ProStep2Market do** It analyzes trader behavior and performance to improve discipline and decision making using AI driven insights.

**Do you provide trading signals** No. The platform does not provide buy or sell signals, copy trading, automated trading, or financial advice.

**How do I connect my account** Use MT5 read only connection or upload CSV trade history. Follow the onboarding MT5 setup guide.

**Is my data secure** Yes. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Trading connections are read only.

**Can I use the platform without connecting MT5** Yes. Manual uploads and CSV imports are supported.

**What is Edge Score** A gamified performance score that measures discipline, risk management, emotional stability, and execution quality.

**Can brokers use this platform** Yes. Enterprise plan supports white label and broker analytics.
