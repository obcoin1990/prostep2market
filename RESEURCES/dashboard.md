## Dashboard Widgets
**Edge Score Card** current score; trend; rank; quick tips to improve score  
**Emotional Risk Meter** live indicator of emotional pressure and recommended actions  
**Weekly Performance** PnL; win rate; average RR; session breakdown  
**AI Alerts** recent behavioral warnings and suggested actions  
**Trade Statistics** pair performance; best session times; worst conditions  
**Session Analytics** heatmap of trading hours and performance by session
