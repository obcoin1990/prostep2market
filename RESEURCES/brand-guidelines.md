# Brand Guidelines

## Meta
**Title** ProStep2Market — Brand Guidelines  
**Description** Visual identity, voice, and usage rules derived from the ProStep2Market logo.

---

## Brand Essence
**Positioning** AI-powered trader development and performance intelligence ecosystem.  
**Mission** Help traders understand themselves before trying to understand the market.  
**Brand Promise** Discipline, clarity, and measurable growth for retail traders.

---

## Core Values
- **Clarity** clear insights, no noise  
- **Discipline** measurable progress and accountability  
- **Empathy** human-first coaching and psychology-aware feedback  
- **Professionalism** enterprise-grade security and design

---

## Tone of Voice
- **Primary** confident, calm, direct  
- **Secondary** encouraging, analytical, precise  
- **Do** use short, active sentences and concrete actions  
- **Don’t** use hype, vague promises, or trading signals language

---

## Logo Usage Rules

### Do
- Use the **full color** logo on white or very light backgrounds.  
- Use the **reversed** white logo on dark backgrounds.  
- Keep clearspace equal to the height of the "2" in the mark.  
- Use SVG for responsive and crisp rendering.

### Don’t
- Don’t change the logo colors.  
- Don’t stretch, skew, or rotate the logo.  
- Don’t place the logo on busy backgrounds without a solid container.  
- Don’t add drop shadows, outlines, or effects that alter the mark.

### Color Combinations
- **Primary hero**: white background, Brand Red CTA, Brand Green micro accents.  
- **Dark hero**: black background, reversed logo, Brand Red CTA with white text.
